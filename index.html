Who We Are

The Front Range WELS churches are congregations of the Wisconsin Evangelical Lutheran Synod located along Colorado's Front Range. We are united by a shared confession of faith rooted in the Bible and the Lutheran Confessions.

WELS is a group of nearly 330,000 men, women, and children in nearly 1,200 congregations united by a common faith in Christ's saving love. Our Front Range congregations serve communities from Fort Collins to Colorado Springs, offering worship, Bible study, fellowship, and outreach.
